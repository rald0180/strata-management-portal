Committee Roles

The Strata Committee is responsible for managing the Owners Corporation and ensuring the smooth operation of the building.

Key Roles

  • Treasurer: Responsible for managing the finances, budgets, and levies of the Strata.
  • Secretary: Handles administrative tasks such as maintaining records and issuing notices.
  • Chairperson: Leads meetings, ensures compliance with the Strata Act, and represents the committee.

Other members may assist with specific tasks, but the committee can only have up to 9 members.
